Digital playtest of the political rules (docs/prototypes/selectorate-game.md, GDD §4.5). You are the ruler: split the budget between the war, your cronies, and the people — and survive 12 years. Rules crib sheet
Revenue = planets × 2 × tax. Tax grievance +0/+2/+5; bloc anger −0/−1/−3.
War: pressure 1d3 + turn÷4 (+1 in revolt) vs 0.6×military×skim + 0.15×public (max 1.5). Skim = 1 − 0.06 per individual seat. Front ≥14: planet lost (−2 revenue, −10 every seat, grievance +10). ≤4: planet taken.
Seats: drift −3/turn. Individuals: +8×(private÷W − 1.0). Blocs: +6×(public−4)÷4, −4 on reverses (front +2). Satisfied at 50+.
Grievance: +1/turn, −1.2×public. 60+: −1 revenue. 80+: −2 and front pressure +1.
Loyalty = min(1, S% ÷ 10W). Crisis when satisfied seats < 50% (+10pp for 2 turns after any regime action or failed plot): dissatisfied seats move with chance anger×(1−loyalty) — but blocs ignore loyalty (voting is free). Movers outnumber the rest → you're gone. Failed plot: ringleader purged, the rest bought off (+10).
Survive 12 turns to win. Score = treasury + 5×planets + (20 − front).
