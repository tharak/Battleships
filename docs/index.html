Successor Stars working title

A grand strategy game of galactic war and political survival, inspired by Legend of the Galactic Heroes. The empire is dead; you are one of its heirs.

▶ Battle rules prototype v0.2 Playable hex battle testbed — 9 scenario tests of the formation counter-wheel, facing arcs, morale, command radius, and two-state supply, at 5/9/12 squadrons a side. Play either side vs the AI, hotseat, or spectate. Game design document Vision, selectorate-theory politics, fleet combat model, and the phased solo-dev roadmap.
